# Future AI Integration Guide

The AI Knowledge Base and service rules prepare for future FAQ answers, customer intake, Retail/Project classification, and quote drafts. This builder includes no model call, webhook, WhatsApp automation, message sender, or final-quote delivery.

Future final-send eligibility requires an allowed service, Fixed price, complete required information, no discount, no site visit, amount within the service ceiling, Retail type, Need Boss Approval = false, and no unclear request. Any failure means draft and human handover.

Operationally, v1 still requires boss approval for every final quote. Project quotes and variation orders always hand over.
